### Legend & Symbol Explanation
Each character in the **Daily Threat Profile** represents a single 24-hour UTC calendar day, showing the total merged ("Union") threat duration for that region:
* **`.` (dot)**: 0 to 3 hours of active sirens.
* **`-` (dash)**: 3 to 6 hours of active sirens.
* **`~` (tilde)**: 6 to 12 hours of active sirens.
* **`=` (equals)**: 12 to 18 hours of active sirens.
* **`#` (hash)**: 18 to 24 hours of active sirens (near-constant threat).

*The timeline is displayed chronologically from left (oldest date) to right (most recent date).*
